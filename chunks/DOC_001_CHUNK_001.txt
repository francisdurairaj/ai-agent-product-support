# Sony WH-1000XM5 Headphones - Reset & Pairing Guide
## Full Document

To initialize the headset to factory settings: 
1. Turn off the headset. 
2. Press and hold the (Power) button and the (NC/AMB) button simultaneously for about 7 seconds. 
3. The indicator (blue) will flash 4 times. 
4. This operation resets volume settings and deletes pairing information. 



To enter Pairing Mode: Press and hold Power for 5 seconds until you hear 'Bluetooth Pairing'.
