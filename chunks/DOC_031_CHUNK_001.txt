# Sonos Arc - HDMI eARC & Trueplay
## Full Document

No Sound: Ensure HDMI cable is plugged into the TV port labeled 'ARC' or 'eARC'. Enable CEC (Viera Link/AnyNet+) in TV settings. 

Trueplay: 
1. Go to System > [Room Name] > Trueplay. 
2. Remove phone case. 
3. Walk around the room waving the phone slowly as prompted.
