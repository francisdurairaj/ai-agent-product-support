# DJI Mini 3 Pro - RC Pairing & Calibration
## Full Document

Link Remote: 
1. Power on Drone (Press once, then hold). 
2. Power on Controller. 
3. Hold Drone power button for 4 seconds until it beeps. 
4. In Controller settings, tap 'Pair'. 

Compass Error: Move away from metal (cars, rebar). Rotate drone 360 horizontally, then 360 vertically.
