# Philips Hue Bridge - Internet & Light Sync
## Full Document

LED Status: 
1. First LED (Power): Must be solid. 
2. Second LED (Network): Solid means connected to router. If blinking, check Ethernet cable. 
3. Third LED (Internet): Solid means cloud connection. If blinking, router has no internet access. 

Factory Reset: Use a pin to press the button on the bottom for 10 seconds (Erases all scenes).
