# Breville Barista Express - Pressure Gauge
## Full Document

Pressure Low (Under-Extracted): 
1. Grind is too coarse. Move dial to a LOWER number (Finer). 
2. Increase dose amount. 
3. Tamp harder (approx 30lbs). 

Pressure High (Over-Extracted): 
1. Grind too fine. Move dial to HIGHER number. 
2. Decrease dose.
