# Logitech MX Master 3S - Flow & Connectivity
## Full Document

To pair with a second computer: 
1. Turn the mouse over. 
2. Press the Easy-Switch button to select channel 2. 
3. Press and hold the Easy-Switch button for 3 seconds until the LED blinks fast. 

Troubleshooting Lag: If using the Logi Bolt receiver, ensure it is plugged into a USB 2.0 port or use a USB extension cable to prevent USB 3.0 interference.
