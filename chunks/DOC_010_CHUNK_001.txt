# Fitbit Charge 5 - Sync & GPS Issues
## Full Document

If the screen is unresponsive (Black Screen): 
1. Connect to the charging cable. 
2. Press the button on the flat end of the USB connector 3 times within 8 seconds. 
3. Wait for the Fitbit logo. 

GPS: Ensure 'GLONASS' is enabled in the Exercise app settings for better tracking in urban areas.
