# Anker 737 Power Bank - Trickle Charge
## Full Document

Trickle Charge Mode (Green LED): 
1. Double press the button. The small LED turns green. 
2. Use this for low-power devices like AirPods or smartwatches to prevent the bank from auto-shutting off. 
3. Press once to exit mode. 

Screen Reset: If screen % is inaccurate, fully deplete the bank to 0% and recharge to 100%.
