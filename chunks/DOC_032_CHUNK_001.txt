# Oura Ring Gen 3 - Charger Troubleshooting
## Full Document

Charger LED: 
1. Pulsing White: Charging. 
2. Solid Green: Fully charged. 
3. Blinking Red: Error. Take ring off, tap charger on table firmly to reset internal connectors, place ring back on. 

Soft Reset: Place ring on charger. Tap the ring on the table (while on charger) 3 times.
