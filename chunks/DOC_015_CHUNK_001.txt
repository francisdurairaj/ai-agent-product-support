# Instant Pot Duo - 'Burn' Message & Sealing
## Full Document

BURN Message: 
1. Press Cancel. Release steam (Vent). 
2. Open lid. Check inner pot for scorched food. 
3. Deglaze pot with water. 
4. Layer ingredients: Do not put thick sauces (BBQ/Tomato) at the bottom; place them on top of meat/veg.
