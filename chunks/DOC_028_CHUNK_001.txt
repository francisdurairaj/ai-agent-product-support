# Keychron K2 Keyboard - Bluetooth Switching
## Full Document

Pairing: 
1. Switch side toggle to 'BT'. 
2. Hold Fn + 1 for 4 seconds until the key flashes rapidly. 
3. Search for 'Keychron K2' on device. 

Mac/Win Mode: Use the side toggle. In Windows mode, the 'Option' key becomes 'Alt' and 'Command' becomes 'Windows'.
