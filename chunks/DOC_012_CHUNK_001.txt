# Canon G3000 Series - Ink System & Reset
## Full Document

Error 5B00 (Ink Absorber Full): 
1. Turn off. Hold Power + Stop buttons. 
2. Release Stop, keep holding Power. 
3. Press Stop 5 times. Release Power. 

Air in tubes: Perform 'System Cleaning' (takes 10 mins). Ensure tank valves are vertical (Open).
